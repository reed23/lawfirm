Murphy Grantland’s mission is to provide high-quality legal services with uncompromising integrity and uncommon value. Murphy Grantland is experienced in practicing before South Carolina state and Federal courts at the trial and appellate levels,and offers our clients the opportunity to work with attorneys who have diverse legal experience in a variety of practice areas. Murphy & Grantland’s appellate practitioners have extensive experience handling state and federal appeals in a wide range of practice areas. We provide our clients with strategic counseling on complex legal issues by reviewing the record critically, identifying and briefing the legal issues and then presenting an effective oral argument to the panel of appellate judges. Our appellate team includes attorneys who previously served as law clerks for the judges and justices of the South Carolina Circuit Court, the South Carolina Court of Appeals, the United States District Court for South Carolina, and the United States Court of Appeals for the Fourth Circuit..

Experienced in commercial and residential construction claims, Murphy Grantland’s construction litigation team defends: General contractors, Developers, Product manufacturers, Subcontractors, Design professionals, Home Owners’ Associations, and Horizontal Property Regimes. Our attorneys can provide you with knowledgeable legal counsel in the areas of: Architectural and engineering malpractice, Construction defect claims, Construction site accidents, and Contract disputes. We are also actively involved in insurance coverage disputes relating to construction design and defect on behalf of insurers in South Carolina.

Murphy Grantland offers a team of skilled attorneys who are experienced in a broad variety of insurance policy and coverage issues.. Our coverage attorneys are well-versed in the evolving landscape of insurance law in South Carolina, and have lectured and taught on the nuances and idiosyncrasies of insurance coverage issues in South Carolina.

Not every case goes to trial. In fact, in today’s litigation landscape, the vast majority of civil lawsuits never make it to a jury. There are a host of reasons that alternative dispute resolution (ADR) has become the primary means by which civil disputes are resolved. From a practical standpoint, arbitration or mediation reduces costs and eliminates the time and stress of a trial.

Murphy & Grantland represents a wide array of manufacturers, suppliers and retailers in litigation involving allegedly dangerous or defective products. Our experienced and knowledgeable attorneys provide simple and complex legal advice on products liability cases including but not limited to: Building Materials, Tires, Chemicals, Consumer Products, Heavy Equipment, Heating & Air Conditioning, and Industrial Products.
