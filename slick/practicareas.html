Rogers E. Harrell, III

Rogers Harrell is an associate with Murphy Grantland. He concentrates his legal practice on civil defense of insurance companies, businesses and individuals in a wide variety of civil matters. Prior to joining the firm, Rogers was a law clerk for the Honorable R. Knox McMahon of the South Carolina Circuit Court. Rogers graduated from The Citadel, where he received a Bachelor of Arts in Political Science. After graduating from The Citadel, Rogers worked in the construction and manufacturing industry in Charleston, South Carolina for more than a decade. He attended the Charleston School of Law where he graduated with honors. While in law school, Rogers served as a faculty research assistant, a senator in the Student Bar Association and was a member of Phi Delta Phi. Rogers is a proud native of Kingstree, South Carolina. He and his wife, Kimberly, have three beautiful children, Margaret, Edward and Thomas. Rogers enjoys golf, outdoors, cooking, history and reading. EDUCATION
