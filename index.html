Our Firm

In 1997, J.R. Murphy & John Grantland founded a law firm with one mission in mind: To provide the highest quality of legal services with uncompromising integrity and uncommon value. Since then, Murphy & Grantland has grown from two attorneys, a paralegal, and a receptionist to 18 attorneys and a full-fledged legal and office support staff. After outgrowing three different office locations, Murphy & Grantland built its present location at 4406-B Forest Drive in Columbia in 2005, where it is uniquely positioned to serve all of South Carolina from this central location in the Capitol City.
